A social game of human pacman.

In a Nutshell

Map attack is a geo game you can play anywhere on planet earth. You make your own board by placing dots worth different amounts of points onto a map. When you're ready to play, two smart-phone-weilding teams (red and blue) gather up all the dots to collect points. It's crazy. But awesome.

1. Make a Board

Making your own board is really easy. Just sign in with Twitter and click the "new board" button to start a new board.
